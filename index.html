Projects

🔬 Molecular Docking Study on Lung Cancer Targets

Objective:
To investigate the binding affinity of β-carboline derivatives against key oncogenic targets (ALK, BRAF, PI3K, MET) involved in lung cancer.

Methods:
Molecular docking and virtual screening were performed using PyRx and Discovery Studio. Drug-likeness and ADME properties were evaluated using SwissADME.

Results:
Several compounds demonstrated strong binding affinity with target proteins, indicating potential inhibitory activity and favourable pharmacokinetic properties.

Tools Used:
